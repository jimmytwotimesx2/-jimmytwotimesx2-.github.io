Top 10 Gangster Movies

#9: Goodfellas (1990)

An epic tale of a mob associate rising through the ranks, directed by Martin Scorsese.

#8: The Godfather (1972)

Francis Ford Coppola’s masterpiece about a powerful Italian-American crime family.

#7: The Godfather Part II (1974)

A sequel that many consider even better than the original, chronicling the Corleone family saga.

#6: Scarface (1983)

The story of Tony Montana’s rise and fall, starring Al Pacino in a legendary performance.

#5: Pulp Fiction (1994)

Quentin Tarantino's non-linear crime masterpiece filled with unforgettable characters.

#4: The Departed (2006)

A gripping tale of undercover cops and moles in Boston's Irish mob, directed by Scorsese.

#3: Reservoir Dogs (1992)

A heist gone wrong and the fallout between its participants, another Tarantino classic.

#2: Casino (1995)

Set in the glitz and corruption of Las Vegas, Scorsese delivers another mobster epic.

#1: The Untouchables (1987)

The story of Eliot Ness taking down Al Capone, starring Kevin Costner and Robert De Niro.
